About jAlbum

jAlbum is a desktop application that converts your photos into stunning web photo albums you can upload to any site. With us you don't have worry about privacy – we never sell our user's data.

We are selling jAlbum Licenses, which are good for those who run their own websites, and Storage plans (accounts) which also contain storage space on jalbum.net. Useful if you don't have your own site.
